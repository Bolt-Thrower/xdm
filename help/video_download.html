Downloading Videos With XDM

Saving YouTube videos:

  • Goto XDM Menu-> File ->YouTube Downloader.
  • Paste the YouTube page's address

Downloading videos from Metacafe,Google Video,Daily Motion or any other website:

  • Configure browser integration.  Please refere to 'Browser Integration'page
  • Play the video in browser.
  • Download video option will be available

NOTE:

  • Make sure XDM is running while playing the video in Browser.
